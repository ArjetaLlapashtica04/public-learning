My Learning Journey

Hello and welcome! I’m Arjetë Llapashtica, a final-year Computer Engineering student at UMIB (Universiteti i Mitrovicës “Isa Boletini”). This website is part of my public learning journey.

As part of a course called Lab Course, which emphasizes project-based teamwork, my team and I developed an interactive web platform named Kosovo Travel Guide.

This platform supports tourists exploring Kosovo by offering a dynamic, user-friendly, and AI-enhanced experience. It integrates real-time data, personalized recommendations, and gamified planning tools to make travel easier and more enjoyable.

Through this site, I document my personal learning process—detailing what I am building, learning, and improving. It serves as a space to track progress, reflect on challenges, and share insights as I develop skills in frontend and backend development, UX design, and collaborative software engineering.

Feel free to explore the notes and follow along on my journey!
