My Learning Journey

Welcome! I'm Arjetë Llapashtica, a final-year Computer Engineering student at UMIB (Universiteti i Mitrovicës "Isa Boletini"), and this website is all about my public learning journey.

A big part of what you'll see here comes from my "Lab Course," where teamwork and project-based learning are key. My team and I built Kosovo Travel Guide, an interactive web platform aimed at helping tourists explore Kosovo. We've made it dynamic, user-friendly, and boosted it with AI for real-time data, personalized recommendations. Our goal? To make travel easier and more enjoyable!

This site is where I track my progress, sharing insights as I develop skills in frontend, backend, and UX design.

Feel free to say hi, share your thoughts, or even give suggestions!
